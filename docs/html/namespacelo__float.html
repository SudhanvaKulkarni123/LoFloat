◆ Rounding_Mode

enum lo_float::Rounding_Mode : uint8_t

Defines different rounding strategies for floating-point operations.

Enumerator
RoundToNearestEven 

Round to the nearest representable value.
If equidistant between two representable values, round to the one with an even least significant bit.

RoundTowardsZero 

Round toward zero (truncate fractional part).

RoundAwayFromZero 

Round away from zero (always go to the numerically larger magnitude).

StochasticRounding 

Use stochastic rounding to decide how to round the fractional part.

RoundToNearestOdd 

Round to the nearest representable value.
If equidistant, pick the one whose least significant bit is odd.

RoundDown 

Round down (toward -∞), sometimes called "floor" for positive values.

RoundUp 

Round up (toward +∞), sometimes called "ceiling" for positive values.

RoundTiesToAway 

Round ties away from zero.
If exactly halfway between two representable values, pick the one with larger magnitude.
